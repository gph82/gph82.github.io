'Obelisks': A new lifeform?

February 2025 · 

Science at the Cervantes Institute in Berlin

CERFA collaborates closely with the Instituto Cervantes Berlin in their Science Program, and I was invited to moderate a conversation from Dr. María López Galiano and Dr. Selma Gago-Zachert as they explore the discovery of “obelisks” (check Dr. López Galiano’s Cell Paper), a type of newly identified, ultra-simple infectious agents living within human-associated bacteria.
