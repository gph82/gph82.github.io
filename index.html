Dr. Guillermo Pérez-Hernández

I am a senior research associate at Universität des Saarlandes (Oct. ‘25), working remotely from Berlin, where I worked at Charité Universitätsmedizin Berlin until recently.

My research focuses on conformational dynamics of cell-signaling proteins, primarily
G Protein-Coupled Receptors (GPCRs), G proteins, and kinases, through long-timescale molecular dynamics simulations and statistical modelling.
